About Me

I am a young and passionate pixel addict who was born with a camera in her hand.

I am passionate about making things more usable and easier to handle ... and as much as I like to work I also like lifting weights and enjoy travelling.

I am working as a Software Engineer at IBM UK in an agile development environment. Since completing the UX certification with CareerFoundry in 2016, I was able
to join and lead a couple IBM Design Thinking workshops, working and researching on small projects and attending Design conferences, to stay up to date and network
with other creative people.
